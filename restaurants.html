Taste the local flavour. Kotagiri is Badaga country — ask your homestay host about home-cooked Badaga meals, and don't leave without a cup of single-estate Nilgiri tea (chocolate, cardamom and ginger varieties are local favourites). Most sit-down places cluster around Johnston Square and Donnington Road, with Silvertip's venues out toward Aravenu.
