📱 Customer Feedback - QR Setup

How to Use This Form

Step 1: Print this page

Step 2: Place QR code stickers at:

  • Table stands (dine-in)
  • Delivery bags (takeaway)
  • Receipt printer (on bills)
  • Entrance (for post-visit feedback)

Step 3: Customers scan QR → fill form on phone

Step 4: You review feedback weekly

✓ Form responses are collected in Google Sheets

🔧 Setup Instructions

  1. Create Google Form:
    • Go to forms.google.com
    • Click "Create → Blank Form"
    • Add questions from section below
    • Get the "Share" link (shortened)
  2. Generate QR Code:
    • Go to qr-code-generator.com
    • Paste your Google Form link
    • Download QR code image
    • Print as stickers (100 recommended)
  3. Place QR Codes:
    • Small stands on each table
    • Delivery bag stickers
    • Bottom of every receipt
    • Main entrance signage
  4. Review Responses:
    • Google Form auto-collects responses
    • Review responses weekly (Mondays)
    • Export to CSV for analysis
    • Use findings in menu/operation decisions

📋 Google Form Questions

Copy these questions into your Google Form (in order). Use the question types specified.

Question 1 (Short Answer)

What's your name or order number?

Purpose: Track repeat customers

Question 2 (Linear Scale 1-5)

How would you rate the food quality? (1=Poor, 5=Excellent)

Purpose: Track quality consistency

Question 3 (Linear Scale 1-5)

How would you rate the service speed? (1=Slow, 5=Very Fast)

Purpose: Track service efficiency

Question 4 (Multiple Choice)

Would you order from us again?

Options: Yes | Maybe | No

Purpose: Track repeat intention

Question 5 (Checkboxes - Multi-select)

What did you like most? (Check all that apply)

Options: Food Quality | Taste | Portion Size | Price | Service | Ambiance | Cleanliness | Other

Purpose: Identify strengths to promote

Question 6 (Checkboxes - Multi-select)

What could we improve? (Check all that apply)

Options: Wait Time | Food Quality | Portion Size | Price | Staff Behavior | Cleanliness | Menu Variety | Delivery Speed | Other

Purpose: Identify improvement priorities

Question 7 (Long Answer)

Any other comments or suggestions?

Purpose: Capture detailed feedback

📊 Weekly Review Process

Monday Morning Review (15 mins)
  1. Export Google Form responses to CSV
  2. Count star ratings (food quality, service speed)
  3. Identify 3 top complaints from Q6
  4. Identify 3 top strengths from Q5
  5. Review written feedback (Q7)
  6. Share summary in team meeting
Action Items (Based on Feedback)
  • Quality Issues: Address in next day's prep (if food quality < 4/5)
  • Service Issues: Adjust staffing/process (if speed < 4/5)
  • Repeat Intention: If "No" increases, trigger menu/service audit
  • Consistent Feedback: Implement improvement within week
